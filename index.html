Our Vision

The full title of this organization is Odenaansan Dakonaan.

Odenaansan is an Anishinaabe term meaning “village or little place where my heart is”. Dakonaan is a word provided by Anishinaabe knowledge keepers – it means “to hold for others”. With these two words odenaansan dakonaan is described.

To ensure that future generations can inherit a healthy world, Odenaansan is beginning a ten-year project to put into place infrastructure to restore our relationship with water to one that is sustainable and sacred.
